Events

1. Penalty Kicks: Best of 5 soccer penalty kicks. Competitors rotate between being shot taker and being goalie. Coin flip to decide who goes first

2. One vs. One Basketball: Best of Three games of one on one. Each game will be played until 11, with shots being ones and twos. Must win by two

3. Catching Footballs: With a designated qb, competitors will take turn catching footballs with one hand, first to drop x passes is the loser

4. Golf Putting Contest: Competitors will go back and forth in a sudden death putting contest. First one to make a putt in a designated hole wins

5. Billiards: Best of three games. Standard Billiards rules.

6. Chopped Cooking competition: Standard Chopped rules. Each competitor will get a set amount of time to prepare a dish using mystery ingredients. Judges will taste both dishes and decide the winner

7. Boxing with Socker Boppers: Best of three rounds, judges will determine who won each round after a set time

8. Real Life Fortnite: Simple fortnite setup. First to eliminate their opponent wins

9. One Bounce: Best of three games. Standard one bounce rules

10. Eating: Pizza Madness! Competitors will go to an all you can eat pizza buffet, and whoever can eat the most slices of pizza in a set amount of time will win

11. Fans Choice!

As you can see, we still need a 11th event. If you have an idea for an event, please send an email to: jrleuffen@aim.com. Please provide the name of the event, as well as the details/rules for that event
