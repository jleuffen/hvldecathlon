Jared Leuffen: Biography

Born Jared Reading Leuffen, he is the youngest child of Eric and Deborah Leuffen. He has an older sister, Kendall Leuffen and a dog, Maggie. Like Hinkle, Jared also grew up on the legendary Steeplechase Drive in Reading, PA. In his youth, he was also a member of the three Jared's that grew up on Steeplechase. As he got older, he began to hang out and become close with both the other Jared's, as well as another Steeplechase legend, Dylan Evanish. He enjoyed playing almost any sport with the neighborhood kids, including pickup basketball, and wiffleball. As he grew up, he developed many nicknames to distinguish himself from the other Jared's, including J Leuff, Leuff Dawg, or simply Leuffen. He would go on to attend school in the Exeter School District for his entire early school career.

In High School, Leuffen was a member of the Soccer team. Primarily a defender, he was part of the county championship team his freshman year. He would go on to play soccer throughout his high school career, making new friends along the way. He would also go on to have a successful academic career, graduating with honors and in the top 8% of his class. After many college visits and many tough decisions, Leuffen decided to attend the University of Pittsburgh, where he currently is today.
